Wisdom — Chapter 12

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verse 4

Verse 10

Verse 18

Gods power being Almightie is only limited by his wil.

Verse 24

Serpents, hares, moles, and like beastes, which seeme not only superfluous in the world, but also hurtful, yet were esteemed as Gods.

Verse 27

Knowing him to be the only true God, by whom they saw their false Gods destroyed, yet they did not serve him as God.
