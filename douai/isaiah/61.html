Isaias — Chapter 61

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1
  2. 6

Verse 1

Our Saviour was not, neither needed to be visibly annoynted, as Aaron, David, & others were; but invisibly by God, with oyle of gladnes, above al others, with the Holie Ghost, and with power.

Verse 6

It was a griefe and sorow to the Apostles & first Preachers of Christ, that both the Iewes departed from God, & Gentils stil folowed idolatrie, but shortly after, manie were converted.
