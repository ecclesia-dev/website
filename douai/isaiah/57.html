Isaias — Chapter 57

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1
  2. 21

Verse 1

Iust men dying seeme to the wicked to perish. But they are gathered to the happie societie of other blessed soules. And commonly God so taketh away the iust, when he wil punish the wicked people, that they may not see the general calamitie of others.

Verse 21

Those that persist obstinate can haue no remission.
