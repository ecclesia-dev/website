Isaias — Chapter 19

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1
  2. 19

Verse 1

When our Saviour was carried in his infancie by his mother into Aegypt, the idols of that countrie lost their power. And the inhabitants were specially blessed, and afterwards very many believed in Christ, and sincerely served him.

Verse 19

Both Iewes and Christians understand this prophecie of the conversion of the Aegyptians to Christ. For there were sometimes many Christians in that countrie, yea manie most excellent Saints, S. Paul, S. Antonie, S. Hilarion, & innumerable others.
