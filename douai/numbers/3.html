Verse 39

Twentie two thousand.] Immediately before were numbered of Gersonites 7500, of Caathites 8600, and of Merarites 6400, which make in al 22100. Why then are 300 left out of the total summe? Some suppose that the first borne of the Leuites were iust 300, and therfore so manie supplying only their owne places, there remained iust twentie two thousand to supplie the places of the first borne of other Israelites. Howfoeuer this doubt be solued, sure it is, by S. Hieroms iudgement, that these numbers are mysticall. And the iust number of 22 thousand Leuites may signifie (faith Origen homil. 4. in Num.) perfection, required in those that are designed to the particular seruice of God.
