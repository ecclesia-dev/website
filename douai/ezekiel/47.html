Ezechiel — Chapter 47

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1
  2. 9
  3. 13

Verse 1

There is no historie nor probabilitie that waters issued out of the temple reedified by Zorobabel; & therfore this prophecie hath an higher & truer sense, of the Church of Christ, and the water of Baptisme.

Verse 9

S. Iohn saw this riuer of liuing water as clere as chrystal, proceeding from the seat of God, & of the Lambe, and the tree of life yeelding twelue fruites euerie month. Apoc. 22.

Verse 13

Iosephs two sonnes had ech one a whole portion, and so there were twelue tribes besides the Leuites, who had other better meanes then the rest.
