Ezekiel — Chapter 11

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1
  2. 3
  3. 7
  4. 13
  5. 16

Verse 1

The Prophet in Chaldea saw in spirit what was done in Ierusalem.

Verse 3

And so cornered themselues as secure, in Ierusalem: as flesh in the Potte.

Verse 7

The false Prophets scattered warres, but not captiuitie, therfore the Prophet assureth them that the people shal feele both sword & captiuitie.

Verse 13

He lamenteth not the death of the false Prophet, but feared great ruine of the people, seeing this wicked man dye so sodainly.

Verse 16

God stil conservueth his Church from vtter ruine, as the Prophets doe often affirme. Iere. 4 v. 17, ch. 5. v 10. 18. Psal. 88. v. 3. 35.
