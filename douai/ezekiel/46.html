Ezechiel — Chapter 46

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 2
  2. 16

Verse 2

Not only Christian Doctours, but also Rabbi Dauid & other Hebrewes vnderstand this prophecie of Christ the true Messias, and of the sacrifices & rites of his Church: the letter neuertheles alluding to the forme of the old law.

Verse 16

Al workes done by the free choise of God, that is to say done in the state of grace, doe merite eternall reward. But other good workes done in state of mortal sinne are only rewarded temporally in this world, and not in life euerlasting.
