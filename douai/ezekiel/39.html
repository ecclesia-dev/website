Ezechiel — Chapter 39

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 4
  2. 10
  3. 23

Verse 4

Antichrist persecuting the Church in al parts of the world, shal be resisted by some in euerie place, & at last vanquished.

Verse 10

Not with material fire, but with zele & feruour, Catholikes shal resist him, and finally ouercome him.

Verse 23

Gods people were not made captiues by the power of their enemies, as if God could not defend them, but by his permission for punishment of their sinnes.
