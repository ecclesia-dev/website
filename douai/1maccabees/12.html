1 Machabees — Chapter 12

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

⚠ Note on Chapter & Verse Numbers

This content was digitized from the original 1609 Douay (Old Testament) and 1582 Rheims (New Testament) print editions by OCR. The OCR process sometimes confused print page numbers with verse numbers, and may have assigned annotations to the wrong chapter. Chapter and verse labels on this page reflect the OCR output from the original print pagination and may not correspond to canonical Scripture chapter/verse numbers. For canonical reference, consult a standard Douay-Rheims edition. The annotation texts themselves are authentic 1609/1582 Douay-Rheims content.

Verse 1

Simon the fourth general captaine of the Machabees, & high-priest excelled his bretheren in wisdome, by the testimonie of his father.

Verse 2

Sparta the chief citie of Lacedemonia called also Lacedemon, and Taramne.

Verse 6

Spartians otherwise called Lacedemonians (by Iosephus and other writers) descended from Abraham, v.21. and were in great league with the Iewes.

Verse 19

The Spartianes had written this epistle before Onias wrote to them, though it be here placed after.

Verse 21

There remained with the Spartiates old writes of genealogies, as Iosephus supposeth. c. 12. c. 3. li. 13. c 9.

Verse 39

Morally in Tryphonis noted the practise of the diuel: who intending to ouerthrow a King or a Kingdome, first seeketh to deceiue the pastours, and to destroy them: especially by errour or other sinne. For (as S. Gregorie teacheth, ho. 28.) if the pastours life be corrupted, his doctrine wil be contemned.
