1 Kings — Chapter 16

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 7

Verse 7

It is proper to God to see the secret cogitations of mens harts, of himselfe and by his owne power. And glorified Saints know our cogitations by seeing God, in whom al things appeare, that perteine to their state, and for the profit of others. S. Aug. li. de cura pro mortuis, c. 15. S. Greg. li. 12. c. 13. Moral. Prophets being yet mortal, by inspirations doe also see secret cogitations. 1. Reg. 9. 9. Reg. 14. &c.
