Ecclesiasticus — Chapter 21

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1
  2. 29

Verse 1

As a serpent deceitfully approcheth & stingeth the bodie, so al sinnes inuegle and hurt the soule.

Verse 29

Wicked men condemning the diuel or anie other wicked, doe indeed condemne themselues. And to them agreeth that sentence of our Sauiour: By thyne owne mouth I iudge thee, naughtie servant. Luc. 19.
