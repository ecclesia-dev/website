Ecclesiasticus — Chapter 39

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verse 1

Besides actiue life commended in the former chap. it is necessarie that vertuous men vse also meditation & contemplation.

Verse 8

True knowledge of holie Mysteries: without which meditation wil be erronious.

Verse 18

Puritie of soule free from grieuous sinnes, and endowed with vertues.

Verse 20

Humilitie is especially required in contemplatiue persons.

Verse 27

Hope of eternal reward comforteth, & encourageth the seruants of God.

Verse 31

In the meane time God giueth necessaries for this life. Which the good vse rightly to their merite, & the wicked vse euil to their damnation.

Verse 33

Diuels were created in state of grace, & of their owne wil fel from God, & consequently were punished.
