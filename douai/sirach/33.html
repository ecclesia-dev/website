Ecclesiasticus — Chapter 33

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verse 2

3..A man of vnderftanding belieueth the law of God, and the law is fureto him, 4, He that repeareth an interrogation, fhal better {prepare his anfwer, and fo fha) be heard, and thal keepe difcipline,

Verse 5

Men led with passions runne from one vice into an other without ceasing. Especially heretikes runne into manie errours.
