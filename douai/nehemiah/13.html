2 Esdras — Chapter 13

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 4
  2. 31

Verse 4

(a) This Tobias was an Ammonite & a persecuter, c. 14. to whom Eliasib being a-kin (by reason of vnlawful mariages) ioyned in felowship with him wicked lucre: which therfore Nehemias corrected, perfiguring therin our Sauiours zele, who threw out the buyers and sellers out of the temple.

Verse 31

(a) A iust man that hath merited by good workes may pray with great confidence for reward.
