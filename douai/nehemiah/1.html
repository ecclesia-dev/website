2 Esdras — Chapter 1

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1

Verse 1

Nehemias being sent from a King; by his name which signifieth comfort from our Lord; by his building againe the wals of Jerusalem; prefigured our Sauiour, who was sent from God the Father, himselfe being the comforter of mankind, and the sender of another comforter the Holy Ghost, to remaine with his Church.
