2 Esdras — Chapter 4

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1

Verse 1

(a) It was Gods prouidence that the enemies mocking at the reparation of Ierusalem did not furiously resist, til the worke was performed: so sometimes heretikes scoffe at the endeauours of poore Priefts labouring to restore Catholike religion: but whether they scoffe or rage, Gods holy worke proceedeth and prospereth.
