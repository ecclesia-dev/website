1 Paralipomenon — Chapter 23

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 6

Verse 6

Aarons fonnes being consecrated Priests according to Gods ordinance, Leuit. 8. the Leuites to doe other offices about the tabernacle, Num. 3. & 4. King Dauid (with Sadoc the high Priest, & other chief men, 1.14.v.6.) disposed them by lots which should serue by courtes, to sing & play on instruments, as wel in the tabernacle now resting in Ierusalem, v. 26. as in the Temple when it should be built.
