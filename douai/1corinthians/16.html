Verse 2

That is Sunday. Hiero. q. 4. Hedibiæ. So quickly did the Christians keepe Sunday, holiday, and assembled to Diuine Seruice on the same.

Verse 8

The Heretikes & other new-fangled striue among themselues, whether Pentecost signifie here the terme of fiftie daies, or els the Iewes holy-day so called. But it commeth not to their minds that it is most like to be the feast of whitsontide, kept & instituted euen then by the Apostles, as appeareth by the Fathers. See S. Aug. ep. 119. c. 25. & 16. Ambr. in c. 17. Lucæ.
