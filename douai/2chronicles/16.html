2 Paralipomenon — Chapter 16

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 12

Verse 12

(a) Iust punishment with paine of his feet, for iniuriously putting Gods Prophet in fetters v. 10. So God punished him temporally, for that and other passionate sinnes: & he died in good state, for his hart was perfect al his dayes (c. 17.) that is, most part of his life, especially in his last dayes.
