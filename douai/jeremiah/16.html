Verse 1

No prayers of others doe auaile for obstinate & impenitent sinners. Moyses and Samuel, after their death, both could and did sometimes pray for the people. S. Ierom, S. Chrysostom, and S. Gregorie vnderstand this of Moyses and Samuel being in blessed state, where their former great zele and charitie were now greater, and more perfect then in this life.
