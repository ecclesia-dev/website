Jeremias — Chapter 1

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1
  2. 3
  3. 9
  4. 11
  5. 19

Verse 1

Gods clemencie inuiteth to repentance, & his iustice punisheth obstinate sinners.

Verse 3

He Prophecied also in banishment in Aegypt. Ieremie had fiue speciall prerogatiues: He was sanctified in his mothers wombe; a Priest; a Prophet; a perpetuall virgin; & Martyr.

Verse 9

He prophecied not only of the Iewes but also of the Gentils.

Verse 11

God is watchful to performe his word.

Verse 19

God promised not peace in his life, but victorie in his death.
