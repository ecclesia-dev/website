Canticle of Canticles — Chapter 5

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verse 1

The spouse condescending to Gods wil, is wel content to suffer persecution.

Verse 2

Christ againe sheweth his good liking in his spouses patience.

Verse 4

The spouse desireth to rest in meditation.

Verse 6

And vrged by Christs owne example working for al mankind.

Verse 10

Conferring together, the describing the excellencies of Christ.

Verse 16

As the joy of the blessed, so contrariwise the miserie of the damned is marvelous great, and for ever unchangeable.

Verse 17

And resolue to seeke him, wheresoever he be.
