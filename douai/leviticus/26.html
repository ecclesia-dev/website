Leviticus — Chapter 26

Douai-Rheims 1609 · Annotations

These annotations are from the original 1609 Douay Old Testament, the first complete English Catholic Bible translation, produced by English scholars in exile at the English College of Douai. The archaic spelling is preserved.

Verses annotated
  1. 1
  2. 26
  3. 31

Verse 1

Heretikes holding their corrupt course wil needs haue image of Christ, or Saint, to be the grauen thing, which is forbidden in holie Scriptures: & therefore falsly translate Pesel, a grauen image, where indeed it signifieth an image, feared. [cf. Ex 20 annotation on the same error]

Verse 26

This extreme famine fel vpon some of them in Samaria, 4. Reg. 6. vpon others in Hierusalem, 4. Reg. 25. most specially when they were besieged by Titus. Iosephus li. 7. c. 6. de bello Iudaico.
