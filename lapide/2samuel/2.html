2 Samuel — Chapter 2

Cornelius à Lapide SJ · Biblical Commentary

Verse 1

David consulted the Lord before going up to Hebron; he was anointed king over the house of Judah. Lapide notes he waited on God's direction — a lesson for all rulers: seek God before undertaking government.
