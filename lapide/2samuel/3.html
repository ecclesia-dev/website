2 Samuel — Chapter 3

Cornelius à Lapide SJ · Biblical Commentary

Verse 1

There was a long war between the house of Saul and the house of David, but David went on and grew ever stronger, while the house of Saul daily decayed. Lapide: the house of David is the Church, growing through tribulation; Saul's house is the synagogue or heresy, inevitably declining.
