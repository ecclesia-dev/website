Zechariah — Chapter 8

Cornelius à Lapide SJ · Biblical Commentary

Verse 19

Haec dicit Dominus exercituum ieiunium quarti

The four fasts (of the fourth, fifth, seventh, and tenth months) shall be 'joy and gladness and cheerful solemnities to the house of Juda.' À Lapide applies this to the Church's transformation of mourning into celebration: the fasts commemorating Jerusalem's fall are replaced by the feasts celebrating Christ's resurrection. Sorrow becomes joy when the cause of sorrow (sin) is removed.
