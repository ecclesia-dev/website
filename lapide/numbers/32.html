Numbers — Chapter 32

Cornelius à Lapide SJ · Biblical Commentary

Verse 6

Moses rebukes Reuben and Gad for asking to settle east of the Jordan: \"Shall your brothers go to war while you sit here? Why do you discourage the heart of the children of Israel?\" The duty of solidarity — none may exempt themselves from the common struggle.
