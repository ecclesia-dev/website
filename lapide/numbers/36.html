Numbers — Chapter 36

Cornelius à Lapide SJ · Biblical Commentary

Verse 6

The daughters of Zelophehad must marry within their own tribe; this preserves the inheritance of each tribe intact — \"the inheritance of the children of Israel shall not be transferred from tribe to tribe.\"

Verse 13

Final verse: \"These are the commandments and judgements which the Lord commanded through Moses to the children of Israel in the plains of Moab upon the Jordan over against Jericho.\" Conclusion of the Book of Numbers on the edge of the Promised Land.
