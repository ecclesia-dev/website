Psalms — Chapter 15

Cornelius à Lapide SJ · Biblical Commentary

CONSERVA ME DOMINE QUONIAM SPERAVI IN TE. Lapide: Psalm 15 (Michtam of David) — Peter cites this psalm in Acts 2:25-28 as fulfilled in Christ's Resurrection. Lapide: the psalm is literally a prayer of David in danger, but principally and properly a prophecy of Christ. "Preserve me, O Lord, for I have put my trust in Thee." The trust of Christ's human soul in the Father throughout His Passion.
