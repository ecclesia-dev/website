Psalms

Cornelius à Lapide SJ · Biblical Commentary

À Lapide's Psalms commentary is among the crowning achievements of his exegetical corpus, expounding the Psalter as simultaneously the prayer of David, the voice of Christ, and the prayer of the Church. Following Augustine's Enarrationes and Bellarmine's Explanatio in Psalmos, à Lapide treats each Psalm in its literal, Christological, and tropological senses with characteristic patristic depth and practical moral application.
