Psalms — Chapter 123

Cornelius à Lapide SJ · Biblical Commentary

ADIUTORIUM NOSTRUM IN NOMINE DOMINI. Lapide: Psalm 123 — "Our help is in the name of the Lord, who made heaven and earth." The concluding doxology that has become the traditional opening of Catholic liturgical functions. Lapide: "Adjutorium nostrum" — our help (not merely my help but OUR: the communal dimension). The Name of the Lord = the divine nature and perfections. "Qui fecit caelum et terram" = the Creator God, whose power over His creation is the ground of our confidence that He can help us.
