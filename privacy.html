Privacy Policy

We don't collect your data. This page explains exactly what that means for our apps and this website.

Our Apps

DRB Bible and Rosary are fully offline apps. They make zero network connections. There is nothing to explain in a privacy policy because there is nothing happening:

  • No analytics, no crash reporters, no telemetry of any kind.
  • No ads and no third-party SDKs.
  • No accounts, no sign-ups, no email collection.
  • No data leaves your device — ever.

The apps work without an internet connection because they were designed that way from the start. Every byte of content ships inside the app bundle. Your phone could be in airplane mode permanently and nothing would change.

This Website

This website is static HTML served from our own server. There is no JavaScript tracking, no analytics platform, and no cookies.

  • No Google Analytics or any other analytics service.
  • No tracking pixels, no fingerprinting, no session recording.
  • No cookies — not even a cookie banner, because there are no cookies.
  • No CDN tracking — fonts are self-hosted and load from our server, not from Google or any third party.
  • No third-party content of any kind. Every resource loads from ecclesiadev.com.

Our web server keeps standard access logs (IP address, timestamp, page requested) as part of normal server operation. These logs are retained briefly for security purposes and are not used for tracking or analytics.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at github.com/ecclesia-dev or reach us through the App Store listing.
