# Rome: Indulgence Field Guide

*By Isidore — Ecclesia Dev*

The most concentrated treasury of indulgences in the Church. Come to work.

> To gain a plenary indulgence: sacramental confession (within approximately 20 days), holy communion (same day), prayer for the Pope's intentions, and complete detachment from sin.

---

## St. Peter's Basilica
*Rome. The tomb of the Prince of the Apostles. One of the Seven Pilgrim Churches.*

**Plenary indulgence** attached to pious visitation as one of the Seven Pilgrim Churches. During Jubilee years, passing through the Holy Door earns the Jubilee plenary indulgence.

- Visit and pray at the tomb of St. Peter — descend to the Vatican Grottoes
- During Jubilee: pass through the Holy Door
- The papal blessing *Urbi et Orbi* (received in person or via radio/TV) carries its own plenary indulgence under standard conditions

## St. John Lateran
*Rome. The Pope's cathedral as Bishop of Rome. One of the Seven Pilgrim Churches.*

**Plenary indulgence** attached to pious visitation. Jubilee Holy Door indulgence applies during Jubilee years.

- Visit and pray in the basilica
- During Jubilee: pass through the Holy Door

## Scala Sancta
*Rome. Twenty-eight steps tradition holds Christ ascended before Pilate, brought from Jerusalem by St. Helena. Directly across the piazza from St. John Lateran.*

**Plenary indulgence** attached to ascending all twenty-eight steps on one's knees.

- Ascend every step on your knees, praying on each
- The *Sancta Sanctorum* chapel at the top is the ancient private chapel of the popes — pause there

## St. Mary Major
*Rome. The greatest Marian basilica in Rome. One of the Seven Pilgrim Churches.*

**Plenary indulgence** attached to pious visitation.

- Visit and pray before the icon *Salus Populi Romani* in the Borghese (Pauline) Chapel
- During Jubilee: pass through the Holy Door

## St. Paul Outside the Walls
*Rome. Built over the tomb of the Apostle Paul. One of the Seven Pilgrim Churches.*

**Plenary indulgence** attached to pious visitation.

- Pray at the confessio directly over St. Paul's tomb
- During Jubilee: pass through the Holy Door

## The Seven Churches Pilgrimage
*Rome. The circuit of seven ancient pilgrim churches walked by St. Philip Neri's community from the 1550s onward.*

**Plenary indulgence** attached to completing the full circuit with devout prayer at each church.

The seven: St. Sebastian, St. Lawrence Outside the Walls, Holy Cross in Jerusalem, St. John Lateran, St. Mary Major, St. Peter's, St. Paul Outside the Walls. Traditionally walked overnight on the Tuesday before Ash Wednesday; organized pilgrimages run throughout the year.

- Visit all seven churches and pray devoutly at each
- Walking the full route (~12–14 miles) is traditional but not required for the indulgence

## Santa Maria in Aracoeli
*Rome. Ancient Franciscan basilica atop the Capitoline Hill.*

**Partial indulgence** for a devout visit. Any specific plenary grants: **Verify locally** with the basilica or the Apostolic Penitentiary.
