About

We are Catholic developers building free and open source software to serve the traditional faith. Our mission is to create beautiful, faithful tools for priests, religious, and laity who want to deepen their life of prayer and study using the treasures of Catholic tradition.


Every line of code is written for the greater glory of God. No tracking, no ads, no compromise — just faithful tools, freely given.


Instaurare omnia in Christo — to restore all things in Christ.
