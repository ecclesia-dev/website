Projects

  • DRB Bible CLI — The complete Douay-Rheims Bible in your terminal.
  • Rosary App — iOS app for praying the Holy Rosary. Supports the traditional 15-decade form, guided meditations, and novenas.
  • Gospel Quest RPG — A text-based RPG grounded in Catholic teaching. Learn the faith through Scripture, saints, and doctrine.
  • Liturgical Calendar — The traditional Roman liturgical calendar. Feasts, fasts, ember days, vigils, and proper readings for the Extraordinary Form.
  • Nostr Bots — Three automated bots publishing to the Nostr protocol daily: Daily Aquinas (excerpts from the Summa), Divinum Officium (all seven canonical hours), and Traditional Feast Days (feast, rank, and color each morning).
  • Trad Catholic Site — A comprehensive reference for Traditional Catholicism: prayers, catechism, encyclicals, and devotions.
  • Homeschool Planner — Catholic homeschool planning aligned with the liturgical year.

About

We are Catholic developers building free and open source software to serve the traditional faith. Our work is for priests, religious, and laity who want to deepen their life of prayer and study using the treasures of Catholic tradition.

Every line of code is written for the greater glory of God. No tracking, no ads, no compromise — just faithful tools, freely given.

Instaurare omnia in Christo.
